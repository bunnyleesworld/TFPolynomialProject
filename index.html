TensorFlow.js Interactive Project

You're crafting a mystical point generator that magically conjures points on a canvas at the click of a button.
Each point, a dazzling star, dances around based on the precise angles and positions you control.
Using TensorFlow.js, you're training a brainy model that not only predicts the movement of these stars but also learns from every click.
Plus, you're building in some superpowers, like halting predictions with the press of a button and resetting the canvas to start fresh. It's like creating a cosmic drawing tool with a splash of AI sorcery.

How to use the Point Generator on Mobile

Open the Webpage: Access the site on your mobile browser.
Tap to Generate Points: Tap anywhere on the canvas to start generating stars.
Each tap adds a star to the canvas based on your touch position.
Train the Model: Tap the "Generate Point" button to train the model based on the points you've created.
Stop Predictions: Tap the "Stop Predictions" button if you want to halt the model's predictions.
Reset the Canvas: Tap the "Reset Model" button to clear the canvas and start fresh with new points.

How to use the Point Generator on Browser

Open the Webpage: Open your preferred browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.) and navigate to the point generator site.
Drag to Add More Points:Click and drag your mouse around the canvas to continuously add more stars. Your movements will generate new points in real-time.

Additional Tips

Refresh: If the canvas doesn't respond, try refreshing the page.
